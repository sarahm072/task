About

Genio Code Club is a university community group for students interested in computer science. Students from all undergreaduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.


Your Path To Programming Excellence

Our club is a comprehensive programming community offering in-depth courses on various programming languages and development techniques. Members can apply their knowledge through practical workshops and receive guidance from professional mentors. It's the ideal place for those interested in learning programming and enhancing their personal skills in a supportive, interactive environment.
